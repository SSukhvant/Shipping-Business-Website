ABOUT

MASTER GROUP OF COMPANIES

Profile

The Master Group of Companies provides comprehensive logistic solutions designed to carefully co-ordinate and manage every step in your supply chain, optimising processes and delivering value. With over 30 years of experience in various businesses that comprise the integrated solution, the Master Group’s over 2000 & more professionals are today spread over 48 branches and laboratories across India. The group’s national network is closely integrated with worldwide partner networks – global leaders in each field.

Group Companies

Master Marine Services Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1983 over 30 years ago as a small proprietary survey business, surveying cargo and containers in the port of Mumbai. The miniscule survey activity operating from a single office in Mumbai has today blossomed in to a major survey business establishment as MASTER MARINE SERVICES Pvt. Ltd. comprising of 48 offices in all major Indian cities, ports and ICDs. It overseas a wide spectrum of activities ranging from Bulk, Break bulk, Containerised cargo and equipment survey to Stevedoring and handling of Pure Car Carriers, Container Storage and Repairs, CFS management.

HSE & Quality

The Master Group, for its activities of rendering Project Logistics and Heavy Lift Transportation in accordance with its mission, strives at all times in maintaining the highest standards for Quality, Health, Safety and Environment. Therefore, in keeping with the company’s mission, we shall Provide training and resources to employees to maintain QHSE systems.
